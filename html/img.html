Key Components and Features:

Display Technology: Primarily utilizes LED-backlit LCD for bright, energy-efficient viewing,
or OLED for better contrast and thinner designs.Smart TV Functions: Enabled with Wi-Fi
and operating systems (e.g., Android TV, Tizen, webOS) to run streaming apps like Netflix
and YouTube directly, removing the need for external set-top boxes.Connectivity: Includes
HDMI ports for consoles and media players, USB ports, and Bluetooth for audio devices.
Resolution: Ranging from 720p (HD) to 1080p (Full HD), 4K Ultra HD, and 8K, with 16:9 widescreen aspect ratios.
